How do I identify whether the given partial differential equation is heat or wave equation?

Replying to @anonymous_user123

Ms. Kaavyashree T M

To determine if a given equation is a heat or wave equation, focus on the order of derivatives with respect to time. The heat equation involves only the first derivative with respect to time (du/dt), while the wave equation involves the second derivative with respect to time (d²u/dt²).
